Always back up
your iPhone

Beer Pad is backed up as part of a normal iPhone back up. This means that if you regularly back up your iPhone to iCloud or via iTunes, your list of beers will be safely included in that. If you get a new iPhone, just make sure you restore your device from your most recent back up.
